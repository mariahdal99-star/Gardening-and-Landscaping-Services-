🌼 Comprehensive Lily Growing Guide

1. Choosing the Right Lily

Lilies (Lilium spp.) come in many varieties. Beginners should start with Asiatic lilies as they are hardy and reliable.

2. Sunlight Requirements

6-8 hours of direct sun is best. Flowers love sun; roots love shade.

3. Soil Preparation

Soil pH should be 6.0 to 6.8. Well-draining soil is vital to prevent bulb rot.

4. Planting:

15-20cm deep. Pointy end up.

5. Watering:

Deeply at the base once or twice a week.

6. Fertilizing:

Use 10-10-10 when shoots first emerge.

7. Pests:

Monitor for red lily beetles and aphids daily.

8. Deadheading:

Remove spent flowers but keep green stems.

9. Dormancy:

Only cut stems once they turn completely yellow.

10. Division:

Lift and divide bulbs every 3-4 years in Autumn.
