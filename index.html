🌼 Comprehensive Lily Growing Guide

1. Choosing the Right Lily

Lilies (Lilium spp.) come in many varieties. Beginners should start with Asiatic lilies as they are hardy and reliable.

2. Sunlight Requirements

6-8 hours of direct sun is best. Flowers love sun; roots love shade.

3. Soil Preparation

Soil pH should be 6.0 to 6.8. Well-draining soil is vital to prevent bulb rot.

4. Planting:

15-20cm deep. Pointy end up.

5. Watering:

Deeply at the base once or twice a week.

6. Fertilizing:

Use 10-10-10 when shoots first emerge.

7. Pests:

Monitor for red lily beetles and aphids daily.

8. Deadheading:

Remove spent flowers but keep green stems.

9. Dormancy:

Only cut stems once they turn completely yellow.

10. Division:

Lift and divide bulbs every 3-4 years in Autumn.

📅 Lily Growing Calendar (Annual Cycle)

"Timing is the difference between a flower that survives and a flower that thrives."

Month Growth Stage What to Do
JANDormancy / Early growthInspect bulbs for rot. Water sparingly. Apply 10-10-10 fertilizer as shoots emerge.
FEBVegetative growthWater consistently. Remove weeds. Stake tall varieties.
MARRapid leaf growthFeed every 2–3 weeks. Monitor for aphids and fungal disease.
APRBud formationSwitch to high phosphorus (10-30-20). Regular watering is vital.
MAYFlowering beginsEnjoy blooms. Deadhead spent flowers but keep stems intact.
JUNPeak floweringSupport heavy stems. Water during dry spells. Peak care required.
JULLate floweringReduce fertilizer. Remove faded flowers to conserve bulb energy.
AUGPost-bloomLeaves continue photosynthesis to replenish the bulb. Keep foliage green.
SEPFoliage senescenceReduce watering as leaves yellow naturally. Stop fertilizing.
OCTDormancyCut stems only after they turn brown. Lift or divide bulbs if needed.
NOVBulb maintenanceDivide overcrowded bulbs every 3–4 years. Treat with fungicide if storing.
DECPlanting / RestPlant new bulbs. Ensure excellent drainage for the rest period.

🕒 Weekly Care Schedule

EVERY DAY
  • Check soil moisture.
  • Ensure 4–6 hours of morning sun.
  • Remove fallen debris.
EVERY WEEK
  • Inspect for Pests (Aphids, Slugs).
  • Remove yellow/diseased leaves.
  • Rotate pots (if applicable).
