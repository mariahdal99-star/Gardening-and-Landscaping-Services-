🌿 My Little Garden of Memories 🌿

Welcome, loves!

My name is Flowerpower. I have cared for this garden since the spring of 1999. Flowers grow best when someone remembers them. My lilies have finally bloomed, and I'll be sharing their growth through this website. Thank you for all your support. Please don't forget to sign the guestbook before you leave.

🌷 Garden Maintenance Log 🌷

  • 1999: Planted the first lily. It refused to bloom.
  • 2001: The garden remained quiet. Something beneath it did not.
  • 2003: Pruned the hedges. They grew back overnight.
  • 2006: Removed an old scarecrow. It returned before dawn.
  • 2012: Birds stopped singing near the eastern fence.
  • 2020: The garden no longer needs watering after midnight.
  • 2023: Counted twelve lilies. The next morning there were thirteen.
